Questions

Simple answers

Do I need to fill any form to get my money?
No. We find out about the problem in your city on our own and open your claim for you. We check your earnings every day. When you are ready to get paid, we send the money. You do not do anything.
How do you know how much I earned?
When you sign up, you connect your delivery company account. We use that only to check if your money went down during a problem. We never share it with anyone else.
How much money will I get?
It depends on how many days your money was low. 5 days = 70% of your weekly cover amount. 6 days = 85%. 7 days = 100%. The exact amount depends on your plan - from ₹1,400 to ₹8,400.
Can I stop anytime?
Yes. Your plan is weekly. You can stop anytime. If you stop, your cover also stops from the next Monday.
What are SmartWork tips?
Every Monday we send you tips about when and where to work in your city to earn the most that week. Workers who follow the tips earn around 10–15% more each week.

Behind the Tips

What we use to build your tips

Every tip is built from real data - not guesses. Here is exactly what feeds into your Monday report each week.

Platform Order History
We pull order density and peak hour data from delivery companies API's to identify when and where orders are highest in your city.
Swiggy API Zomato API Blinkit API Dunzo API
IMD Weather Forecast
We read your city's 5-day weather forecast from IMD every 15 minutes. We translate the forecast into simple work windows - safe hours, surge hours, stop hours.
IMD Forecast API IMD Nowcast API
City Event Calendar
Festivals, cricket matches, concerts, and local events cause order spikes. We track these city events and alert you days before so you can plan ahead.
City Events NDMA Alerts
Your Own Past Earnings
We look at your personal earning history from the last 4 weeks to give you personalised slot recommendations - not generic advice. Your tips are different from another worker's tips in the same city.
NDMA Risk Advisories
When government disaster alerts are active in your pincode, we switch to safety-first mode. You get a clear advisory to stay home. Your insurance keeps watching while you rest.

Quick answers
Why hasn't my money arrived yet?
Money is sent within 48–72 hours after you qualify. If it has been longer, please call our helpline.
Can I change my UPI ID?
Yes. Go to My Profile and update your UPI ID. The change takes effect from next week.
I worked on multiple apps this week - does it count?
Yes. We add up your earnings from all connected apps to work out your total daily money.
